react in the called direction before the window closes. keys: W/A/D or arrows - or tap the buttons. in foil & sabre, right-of-way decides whether you're attacking or parrying. don't jump the gun.

Fencing is physical chess with a side of adrenaline.

Right-of-way, distance, timing, and the nerve to commit - all decided in fractions of a second under a buzzer and two colored lights. I love that it rewards patience and punishes panic.
